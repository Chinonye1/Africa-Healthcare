The Beautiful Culture of Africa

Embark on Africa's cultural voyage:
Food

West Africa: Jollof Rice and Fufu. North Africa: Couscous and Tagine. East Africa: Injera and Nyama Choma. Southern Africa: Braai and Bobotie.

Dressing
West Africa: Dashikis and Boubous. East Africa: Kanga, Kitenge, and Masai Shuka. North Africa: Majestic Kaftans.
Dance and Music:
Afrobeat: Fela Kuti's rhythm. Highlife: E.T. Mensah's fusion. Traditional Melodies: Talking Drums and Mbira.
Art:
Masks and Sculptures: Dan Masks and Shona Sculpture. Textiles and Beadwork: Kente Cloth and Maasai Beadwork.
Languages:
Linguistic Diversity: Swahili, Hausa, Yoruba.
